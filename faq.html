FAQ

I have purchased a ticket online, how do I use it?

You will receive an email confirmation and receipt of your purchase, attached to that email will be the PDF version of your ticket(s), you will need to print the ticket(s) and have them ready for the attendant at the front gate. We are unable to scan your ticket from your phone. Please contact our office at 760-940-WAVE if you need any assistance. Learn more.

Are lockers available?

Yes, backpack size lockers are available to rent for $5 per day. You will receive a $1 Wave Bonus Splash Buck upon returning your key. Learn more.

Is your park safe?

Yes, all of our lifeguards are highly trained and certified through Star Guard ELITE. Lifeguards are licensed in water rescues, CPR, first aid, AED use, oxygen use, and spinal management. We staff a first aid station located at our Wave Shack. We take pride in the safety of our park! Learn more.
